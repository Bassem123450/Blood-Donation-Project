Who we are

Relife is about connecting lives and sharing life. As a local nonprofit organization,we seek to help our patients and hospitals to provide a steady and reliable blood supply. And we rely on you—volunteer blood donors and blood drive sponsors—to provide the lifesaving blood and blood products needed. We work together at our donor centers and mobile operations throughout the whole country.

Relife takes these blood donations, including red blood cells, platelets and plasma, and processes them in our in-house laboratory. We hold ourselves, and each other, to the highest of standards and pride ourselves on safety and quality.

Objectives

Mission

Organizing blood donation campaigns and improving blood transfusion Services in national hospitals and medical institutions working in the fields of cancer, general health ! And pediatrics Developing an extensive solid reliable data base of donors for different blood types, aiming to offer patients appropriate donors, free of charge.

Goal

Participate in covering the national needs for blood in transfusion, and its derivatives of good quality.
Care about its availability by those in need, especially children suffering from thalassemia major.
Providing a safety guarantee and promote the national use of blood and derivatives.

Vision

Encouraging and spreading education about blood donation among Egyptians of all ages and sectors.
To become the leading world-class blood system in the innovation of new services, technology and research, that positively impacts blood product safety and availability.
